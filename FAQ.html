Q: What do I do if I can't remember my password?

  • Click the Forgot Password link in the Log In page.
  • Enter your username and assign a new password to your account.
  • Confirm the new password by entering it again.

Q: How do I edit my password?

  • Hover your mouse pointer over the Menu in the navigation bar and find the Edit Password option.
  • Enter your username and assign a new password to your account.
  • Confirm the new password by entering it again.

Q: Where can I view and print the daily report?

  • Hover your mouse pointer over the Menu in the navigation bar and find the Print Daily Report option.
  • Click it and wait for the browser to redirect you to a new tab that previews the daily report as a PDF file.
  • To print the daily report, you can either download the PDF file, save it to your computer, and use your printer or click the Print icon at the top of the PDF file preview page to directly invoke the Print confirmation window.

Q: The sample I am searching for does not show up. What do I do?

  • Make sure that the spelling or the format of what you inputted to be searched is correct.
  • Make sure that this sample exists in your records.
  • If none of the above is satisfied, the sample you are looking for must be inexistent.

Q: I can't find my question anywhere on this page. What do I do?

  • Do not worry. You can ask us your question by going to the Help page. Click the Help button located at the bottom of this page and submit your question there.

Receiving ______________________________________________

Q: Where can I view today's received samples?

  • Click the Daily Receiving Table located beside the Receiving Form below the navigation bar.

Q: I entered the wrong input in the form, but I have already submitted it. Can I correct the wrong data?

  • You cannot. Only the Accounting User has the privilege to overwrite the form details.

Accounting ______________________________________________

Q: How do I make changes in the submitted forms?

  • Find the sample that you would like to edit in the Transactions table.
  • Click the button with a gear icon beside the checkbox of that sample. This will take you to a page that allows you to request change on specific data about that sample.
  • Once you click the Override button, the update request will go to the manager to be approved.
  • To cancel the request, click the Cancel button.

Q: How do I mark a sample/s as paid?

  • Select the checkboxes of the sample/s involved and click Proceed to Billing.
  • Enter the necessary details asked in the form in the Billing page then click Confirm Payment.

Q: Can I view old transactions?

  • Yes. Just search for the lab number, date when it was received, or the contractor of the sample. The Search area can be found at right side of the navigation bar.

Releasing ______________________________________________

Q: How do I release the sample test result?

  • Find the sample in the list under the To Be Released tab that you would like to release and click its corresponding Release button.

Manager ______________________________________________

Q: How do I approve an update request?

  • Go to the Pending Updates tab and find the request that you would like to approve.
  • Choose Save to approve the request. The update will then be immediately reflected on all user records.
  • To reject a request, click Cancel.
